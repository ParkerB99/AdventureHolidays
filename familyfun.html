River Rafting in North Wales

Introducing our Family Holiday Adventure Package for White River Rafting in Wales! This exciting adventure is perfect for families looking for an adrenaline-filled vacation experience.

Activity:

White River Rafting is a thrilling water sport that involves navigating fast-flowing rapids in an inflatable raft. This activity is suitable for all ages and experience levels, and our expert guides will ensure that everyone has a safe and enjoyable time on the water.

Itinerary:

Day 1 - Arrival and Check-In: Arrive at our riverside campsite and check into your comfortable accommodations. Enjoy a delicious welcome dinner and get to know your fellow adventurers.
Day 2 - White River Rafting: After breakfast, you will meet your experienced guides who will provide you with all the necessary safety equipment and instructions for the day. You will then embark on your white river rafting adventure, navigating exciting rapids and enjoying the stunning scenery along the way. After the activity, you will return to camp for a well-deserved dinner and restful night's sleep.
Day 3 - Departure: Enjoy a hearty breakfast before packing up and departing from the campsite.

Price:

Our Family Holiday Adventure Package for White River Rafting in England is priced at £350 per person. This price includes two nights' accommodation, all meals, white river rafting activity with equipment and experienced guides, and transportation to and from the river. Additional activities can be added for an extra fee.

Biking through England

Ever wanted to see the beautiful greenscapes of England? Now is your chance! The whole family can experience the countryside on wheels thanks to our latest biking package.

Activity:

Enjoy a family-friendly biking adventure through the scenic countryside of England. This package offers a chance to explore the beauty of rural England while enjoying a peaceful, eco-friendly mode of transportation.

Itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival in London Arrive in London and check-in to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the city or resting up for the biking adventure ahead.
Day 2: London to Oxford After breakfast, you'll meet your guide and travel to Oxford, where you'll begin your biking adventure. You'll start with a leisurely ride through the historic university town of Oxford, stopping to take in the sights and learn about the city's rich history.
Day 3: Oxford to Stratford-upon-Avon Today, you'll continue your biking adventure, cycling through the stunning Cotswolds countryside before arriving in the picturesque town of Stratford-upon-Avon. Along the way, you'll pass through charming villages and enjoy breathtaking views of the rolling hills.
Day 4: Stratford-upon-Avon to Bath On day four, you'll cycle through the stunning scenery of the Cotswolds before arriving in the beautiful city of Bath. Take some time to explore this historic city, known for its Roman Baths, Georgian architecture, and vibrant culture.
Day 5: Bath to Bristol On your final day, you'll cycle from Bath to Bristol, passing through charming villages and idyllic countryside along the way. You'll have the chance to explore the vibrant city of Bristol, known for its street art, music scene, and historic harbor..

Price:

The cost of this family holiday adventure package for peaceful biking in England is £2,500 per person, based on a family of four traveling together. This includes all accommodation, transportation, bike rental, a professional guide, and some meals.
